FAQs

How it works
How do I download the app?

To download the Scoot app, you can search “Scoot” in both the App and Google Play stores. An even simpler way is to click the relevant link at the bottom of this page and you’ll be redirected to the correct page.

Can I find a nearby Scoots?

Yes! You can easily locate nearby Scoots using our app. It shows all available scooters in your vicinity in real time.

Do I need a license to ride?

Most cities don’t require a license, but please check your local regulations before riding.

Safe driving
Should I wear a helmet?

Yes, always wear a helmet! Safety comes first — it’s required in most cities and protects you in case of accidents.

How about the rules & regulations?

Always follow traffic laws, use bike lanes when possible, and respect pedestrians.

What if I damage my Scoot?

Please report any damage in the app. Our team will assess and assist you right away.
